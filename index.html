Metro North Destinations Sleep Confidently

Metro North Destinations is the simplest way to make sure you get that stress-free nap on the train, without having to worry about sleeping through your stop. By using advanced geolocation alogrithms, Metro North Destinations checks how far you are from your stop and wakes you up with plenty of time. Metro North Destinations also keeps your battery in mind, by only using resources when necessary.

Simple to use

Open the app on your mobile phone, choose your line and your station

View distance

As you ride, check how far away you are from your stop

Sleep Confidently

Never worry again about missing your stop while you sleep. Metro North Destinations will wake you up when you get close
